About Nikitha Hospital

[ Hospital Photo Placeholder ]

A Legacy of Trust in Visakhapatnam

Established in 2002, Nikitha Hospital is a premier 60-bed Multi-Specialty Surgical & Bariatric center located at Seethammadhara Junction. For over two decades, we have been at the forefront of providing advanced medical care with a compassionate approach.

We are proud to be the pioneers in several advanced surgical procedures in Visakhapatnam, offering world-class facilities and a team of highly experienced specialists dedicated to your well-being.

  • Rajiv Aarogyasri Empanelled Network Hospital
  • State-of-the-art Modular Operation Theatres
  • 24/7 Emergency & Trauma Care
  • Advanced ICU Facility

Our Specialties

⚖️

Obesity & Bariatric Surgery

Gastric sleeve, bypass, and banding. Vizag's first and highest-volume bariatric centre for sustainable weight loss.

✨

VASER Liposuction

Advanced body contouring for abs, tummy, arms, and thighs. Equipped with Vizag's 1st VASER machine for less pain.

🔬

Laparoscopic Gynaecology

Minimally invasive hysterectomy (TLH), PCOD drilling, ovarian cysts, and endometriosis management.

🤱

Obstetrics & Maternity

Complete pregnancy care, well-equipped labour room, continuous foetal monitoring, and safe delivery protocols.

🧬

Infertility Treatment

Diagnostic laparoscopy, chromotubation test, IVF support, and comprehensive PCOD management.

🏥

General & Laparoscopic

Surgical solutions for gallbladder, appendix, hernia, thyroid, and bowel conditions with faster recovery.

🤖

Robotic Surgery

Advanced robotic-assisted procedures ensuring unmatched precision, smaller incisions, and faster recovery.

🩺

Urology

Treatment for kidney stones, BPH, and stress urinary incontinence managed by our expert on-call specialists.

🦴

Orthopaedics & Trauma

Management of long bone fractures and comprehensive trauma care available round-the-clock.
